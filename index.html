ABOUT US

“If you cut a tree, you kill a life. If you save a tree, you save a life. If you plant a tree, you plant a life.”
- Trees make the environment cleaner and provide us with oxygen. They’re essential for life to flourish.

We plant trees and take care of them as they are the important element of Life.
We will basically provide a wide variety of plants, trees, vegetable plants, seeds for agriculture, fertilizers, agriculture tools and supplies, Garden tools and supplies. Most of the plants we sell will be grown by ourselves and some will be provided by local shopkeepers and farmers. With a convenient location Nature Care Nursery intends to successfully market to the residential customer, as well as contractors. We will encourage local shopkeepers, farmers and gardeners as people prefer online services than visiting the shop personally.
